About Us

Sadagiri, a luxurious five-star hotel nestled in the picturesque city of Kandy, offers a truly opulent experience for discerning travelers. Boasting an exquisite blend of modern elegance and cultural charm, this grand hotel stands as a symbol of sophistication and comfort.

Situated amidst the lush landscapes of Kandy, Sadagiri provides guests with a tranquil escape from the bustling city life. The hotel's architecture reflects a harmonious fusion of contemporary design and traditional aesthetics, creating an ambiance that is both inviting and awe-inspiring.
