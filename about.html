About Sadagiri Hotel

Sadagiri, a luxurious five-star hotel nestled in the picturesque city of Kandy, offers a truly opulent experience for discerning travelers. Boasting an exquisite blend of modern elegance and cultural charm, this grand hotel stands as a symbol of sophistication and comfort.

Situated amidst the lush landscapes of Kandy, Sadagiri provides guests with a tranquil escape from the bustling city life. The hotel's architecture reflects a harmonious fusion of contemporary design and traditional aesthetics, creating an ambiance that is both inviting and awe-inspiring.

The lavish accommodations at Sadagiri are meticulously designed to cater to the highest standards of luxury. Spacious rooms and suites are adorned with elegant furnishings, offering panoramic views of the scenic surroundings. Guests can indulge in world-class amenities, including fine dining restaurants, a rejuvenating spa, and state-of-the-art fitness facilities.

The hotel's commitment to impeccable service is evident in every aspect of the guest experience. Attentive and courteous staff members ensure that every need is met with a genuine smile, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere throughout the property.

Sadagiri's prime location in Kandy allows guests to explore the rich cultural heritage of the city, including iconic landmarks such as the Temple of the Tooth and the Royal Botanical Gardens. Whether savoring gourmet cuisine, relaxing by the poolside, or engaging in cultural excursions, Sadagiri offers an unparalleled blend of luxury and authenticity for an unforgettable stay in the heart of Kandy.

Our Mission
